Bihar Yoga School, Munger

Established in 1964, the Bihar School of Yoga is aimed to impart yoga training and knowledge to householders and sanyasins alike. Since the first Yoga Teacher Training Course, which was conducted in 1968, the school has come a long way to grow into a center of global repute.


The Yoga practiced and taught at the Bihar Yoga School is a synthesis of many approaches. Using the wisdom based on traditional, vedantic, tantric and yogic teachings in conjunction with modern physical and mental sciences, the school endeavors to enhance spiritual, mental and physical well-being of individuals.


In addition to being itself a torchbearer in Yoga practices, the school also guides various projects as well as medical research in association with prestigious establishments, hospitals and organizations.


Modules and programs designed by the Bihar Yoga School are used in a number of sectors, including the education and Government sectors, Defense establishments, private and public sectors including enterprises such as Indian Oil, Coal India Limited, The Steel Authority of India, National Thermal Power Corporation, Oil and Natural Gas and Hindustan Copper Corporation etc.

Accommodation and facilities at Bihar Yoga School

Accommodation is limited and restricted. It is moderately built which is suitable for a seeker to live comfortably. Rooms are furnished with electricity, fans and windows. All the rooms are clean and airy and based on double and triple occupancy with shared bathroom and toilet.


The school has a well-stocked library which house a number of books on Yoga and also the school’s publication on yoga health techniques and other researches. Besides, the school also organizes seminars, campaigns and lecture tours throughout India and the world.


Meals are provided in a simple vegetarian style. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are available in the kitchen area while private cooking is not allowed.

How to Reach Bihar Yoga School

Bihar Yoga School is situated in Munger, which is approximately 6 kilometers from Jalampur Railway Station. On the other hand, Patna-Jai Prakash Narayan Airport is the nearest airport to reach Munger. Taxis, Cabs and buses are available from the airport and the station to take guests to Bihar Yoga School while same are also available from nearby cities like Patna in order to access the institute.
