Services

Our Services

Welcome to Bihar, a state located in the eastern parts of India. Situated on the Gangetic plain, and sharing its borders with Uttar Pradesh on its west, West Bengal on its south and east, and Nepal on its North, Bihar stands at the top when it comes to natural beauty.

Hotel Reservation -

We at Bihar-tourism.com arrange accommodation/lodging facilities in hotels of all categories for individuals as well as groups in cities like Patna, Bodhgaya, Gaya and Rajgir. Our accommodation facilities can also be had across other cities of India. You can book Hotel in Bihar online and we will do the rest for your accommodations, food and lodging.

Package Tours -

We arrange different tour packages based on various themes like - religion, culture, pilgrimage, hill station, beach, wildlife etc. Our allied tour operators are proficient in planning tours with different themes, especially the Buddhist pilgrimage tours.

Car/Coach Rentals -

We provide car and coach rental services that include economy and luxury class vehicles. The cabs and coach services are available to all major destinations of Bihar. We obtain high model cabs and coaches to serve our clients and tourists coming from different parts of the world.
